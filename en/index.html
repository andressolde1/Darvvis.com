Frequently Asked Questions

What is the process for updating my website?

The process includes an initial assessment, implementation of improvements, and functionality testing. Finally, you will receive a detailed report and technical support to ensure your website remains updated and functional.

How often should I update my website?

It is recommended to update your website at least once a year to ensure everything is working correctly and using the latest technologies. If you have dynamic content or an online store, more frequent updates may be necessary.

What are the benefits of keeping my website updated?

Keeping your website updated improves security, loading speed, and ensures compatibility with the latest browsers. It also provides a better user experience, increases visitor retention, and improves your search engine ranking.

How long does a complete update take?

The time varies depending on the complexity and size of the site. Generally, a complete update can take between one and two weeks, including the initial review, implementation of improvements, and functionality testing.
